Twisty Freakshow

Twisty's Freakshow is a haunted house dedicated to spooking our guests. We take you on a tour to see some of the most amazing monsters, Hollywood special fx artists and so much more. Our freakshow has so much more to offer, including our haunted houses and various different monsters. Twisty's Freakshow was first opened in 1977 and has been in business ever since the murder of 1948. We are always opened saturdays, sundays, and mondays during october. We host birthday parties and we offer mazes, bouncy castles and so much more, if you wish to celebrate your childs birthday party you can contact us on the contact page.
